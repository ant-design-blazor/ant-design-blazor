## zh-CN

改变引导相对于目标的位置,共有 12 种位置可供选择。当 `target=null` 时,引导将会在屏幕中央显示。

## en-US

Change the placement of the guide relative to the target, there are 12 placements available. When `target=null`, the guide will be displayed in the center of the screen.
